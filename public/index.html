About the Studio

Real passion for
fabric in motion

Urreal3D is a 3D clothing animation studio run by Stephen R Christian — a 20-year-old B.Tech IT graduate from India who got into 3D animation because he genuinely loves how clothes move.

Over the past 3 years, Stephen has worked with fashion brands and independent designers, building a workflow that's fast, clean, and focused on one thing: making garments look exactly right on screen.

The studio is small by design. Every project goes through Stephen personally — no junior work, no outsourcing, just focused attention on your brief from start to finish.

Common Questions
What do I need to send you to get started? +
For template orders, just send a photo or sketch of the garment, fabric type, and color reference. For custom work, a brief with your brand style, references, and intended use is enough — we'll handle everything from there.
How does payment work? +
50% upfront to start the project, 50% on delivery before final files are shared. We accept PayPal, Wise, and bank transfer. No payment is required before a quote is agreed upon.
Can I use the final files commercially? +
Yes. All delivered files include full commercial usage rights — you can use them in ads, social media, e-commerce, campaigns, print, and beyond. You own the output.
What if I'm not happy with the result? +
Revisions are included in every package. We keep working until you're genuinely satisfied. If after all revisions you're still not happy, we'll discuss a partial refund — your satisfaction matters more than a one-time payment.
Do you offer discounts for bulk orders? +
Yes — orders of 3 or more deliverables get a 10–15% discount depending on the mix. Ongoing monthly retainer arrangements are also available for brands that need regular content.

Terms, Privacy & Policy

Last updated: January 2024 · Urreal3D – Clothing Animation Studio

1. Services & Deliverables

Urreal3D provides 3D clothing animation and render services as described on this website. All deliverables — videos, images, and project files — are specified in the project brief agreed upon before work begins. Any work outside the agreed scope requires a separate quote and approval.

2. Payment Terms

All projects require a 50% deposit before work starts. The remaining 50% is due upon project completion and before final files are delivered. Accepted payment methods include PayPal, Wise (TransferWise), and international bank transfer. All prices are in USD unless otherwise agreed in writing.

For template-based orders under $150, full payment upfront may be required.

3. Revisions & Cancellations

Each package includes a specified number of revision rounds. Revisions are defined as reasonable adjustments within the original brief — not a change of direction or entirely new concept. Additional revisions beyond the included rounds are billed at $25/hour.

If a client cancels a project after work has begun, the deposit is non-refundable. If Urreal3D cancels the project for any reason, the full deposit will be refunded within 5 business days.

4. Intellectual Property & Usage Rights

Upon receipt of final payment, the client receives full commercial usage rights to the delivered files. This includes use in advertising, social media, e-commerce, print, and broadcast. Urreal3D retains the right to display completed work in its portfolio unless the client requests confidentiality in writing before the project begins.

Source files (Blender/CLO project files) are not included in standard deliverables and may be licensed separately.

5. Privacy & Data

When you submit an inquiry through this website, we collect your name, email address, and project details. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and manage your project — it is never sold, shared, or used for marketing without your explicit consent.

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics on this website. No personal data is stored beyond what is necessary for project management.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us via WhatsApp or email.

6. Confidentiality

We understand that some clients need their projects kept private — particularly unreleased collections or brand campaigns. If you require an NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement) before sharing project details, we're happy to sign one. Just mention it in your inquiry.

7. Turnaround & Deadlines

Estimated delivery timelines are stated in each pricing package. These are estimates based on normal workload and assume timely feedback from the client. Delays in client feedback may extend the timeline accordingly. Rush delivery is available for a 30% surcharge — request this at the time of inquiry to confirm availability.

8. Warranties & Limitations

Urreal3D guarantees that all delivered work is original and created by the studio. We are not liable for delays caused by circumstances outside our control (technical failures, illness, etc.) beyond a reasonable amount. Our total liability in any dispute is limited to the amount paid for that specific project.

9. Contact

  • Or use the booking form on this website
